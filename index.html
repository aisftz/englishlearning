English Tenses Overview

Present Tenses

Simple Present

Digunakan untuk kebiasaan, fakta umum, dan situasi permanen.

Rumus: Subject + V1 (s/es untuk he/she/it)

She works at a bank.

They play football every weekend.

Present Continuous

Digunakan untuk aksi yang sedang berlangsung sekarang atau rencana di masa depan.

Rumus: Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

I am studying English now.

We are meeting tomorrow at 5 PM.

Past Tenses

Simple Past

Digunakan untuk aksi yang sudah selesai di waktu tertentu di masa lalu.

Rumus: Subject + V2

She visited Paris last summer.

They watched a movie yesterday.

Past Continuous

Digunakan untuk aksi yang sedang berlangsung di waktu tertentu di masa lalu.

Rumus: Subject + was/were + V-ing

I was sleeping when you called.

They were playing at 8 PM last night.

Future Tenses

Simple Future

Digunakan untuk rencana, prediksi, atau keputusan spontan di masa depan.

Rumus: Subject + will + V1

I will call you tomorrow.

She will be 25 next month.

Going to Future

Digunakan untuk rencana yang sudah dibuat sebelumnya atau prediksi berdasarkan bukti.

Rumus: Subject + am/is/are + going to + V1

We are going to travel to Bali next month.

Look at those clouds! It is going to rain.

Parts of Speech

Nouns & Pronouns

Nouns (Kata Benda)

Kata yang digunakan untuk menyebut orang, tempat, benda, atau konsep.

People: teacher, student, doctor

Places: city, school, hospital

Things: book, car, computer

Pronouns (Kata Ganti)

Kata yang menggantikan noun untuk menghindari pengulangan.

Personal: I, you, he, she, it, we, they

Possessive: my, your, his, her, its, our, their

Reflexive: myself, yourself, himself, herself

Verbs & Adjectives

Verbs (Kata Kerja)

Kata yang menunjukkan tindakan atau keadaan.

Action verbs: run, eat, write, speak

State verbs: be, have, like, know, believe

Auxiliary verbs: do, have, will

Adjectives (Kata Sifat)

Kata yang mendeskripsikan noun atau pronoun.

The beautiful flower

An interesting book

A tall building

Adverbs, Prepositions & Conjunctions

Adverbs

Kata yang mendeskripsikan verb, adjective, atau adverb lain.

She speaks slowly.

It's extremely hot today.

Prepositions

Kata yang menunjukkan hubungan antara noun/pronoun dengan kata lain.

The book is on the table.

We'll meet at 5 PM.

Conjunctions

Kata yang menghubungkan kata, frase, atau klausa.

I like tea and coffee.

She's tired but she can't sleep.

Sentence Structure

Basic Sentence Patterns

Pattern Formula Example
Subject + Verb S + V Birds fly.
Subject + Verb + Object S + V + O She reads books.
Subject + Verb + Complement S + V + C He is a teacher.
Subject + Verb + Adverb S + V + A They live here.
Subject + Verb + Object + Object S + V + O + O She gave me a gift.

Question Forms

Yes/No Questions

Pertanyaan yang dijawab dengan "ya" atau "tidak".

Do you like coffee? → Yes, I do.

Is she coming? → No, she isn't.

Wh- Questions

Pertanyaan yang dimulai dengan kata tanya.

What is your name?

Where do you live?

Why are you late?

Complex Sentences

Compound Sentences

Menggabungkan dua independent clause dengan conjunction.

I wanted to go, but I was too tired.

She studies hard, so she gets good grades.

Complex Sentences

Menggabungkan independent clause dengan dependent clause.

Although it was raining, we went for a walk.

I'll call you when I arrive.

Conditional Sentences

Conditional Type 0

Digunakan untuk fakta umum atau kebenaran ilmiah.

Rumus: If + simple present, simple present

If you heat ice, it melts.

If people don't eat, they get hungry.

Conditional Type 1

Digunakan untuk situasi yang mungkin terjadi di masa depan.

Rumus: If + simple present, will + V1

If it rains tomorrow, we will cancel the picnic.

If you study hard, you will pass the exam.

Conditional Type 2

Digunakan untuk situasi yang tidak nyata atau tidak mungkin di masa sekarang.

Rumus: If + simple past, would + V1

If I had a million dollars, I would buy a house.

If I were you, I would tell the truth.

Conditional Type 3

Digunakan untuk situasi yang tidak terjadi di masa lalu.

Rumus: If + past perfect, would have + V3

If you had studied harder, you would have passed the exam.

If it hadn't rained, we would have gone to the beach.

Real-Life Conversation Scenarios

At the Restaurant

Waiter:

Are you ready to order?

You:

Yes, I'll have the grilled chicken and a lemonade, please.

Waiter:

Would you like any appetizers or desserts?

You:

Just the main course for now, thank you.

Key Expressions:

  • I'll have... - Saya mau pesan...
  • Would you like...? - Apakah Anda mau...?
  • For now - Untuk sekarang

Making a Phone Call

You:

Hello, may I speak to Mr. David, please?

Receptionist:

Sure, hold on a moment. Who's calling, please?

You:

This is Sarah from ABC Company.

Receptionist:

One moment, please. I'll connect you.

Key Expressions:

  • May I speak to...? - Bisa bicara dengan...?
  • Hold on a moment - Tunggu sebentar
  • Who's calling? - Dengan siapa?
